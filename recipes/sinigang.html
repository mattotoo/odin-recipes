Sinigang

Description

Sinigang, a quintessential Filipino soup, is celebrated for its tantalizing blend of tangy and savory flavors. The soup's signature sourness, achieved through ingredients like tamarind or calamansi, is perfectly balanced with a colorful array of vegetables, often including water spinach, radish, eggplant, and string beans. Various protein choices, such as pork, shrimp, or fish, are added to create a hearty and satisfying dish that caters to diverse tastes. Simmered to perfection, sinigang captures the essence of Filipino cuisine, offering a comforting and deeply satisfying culinary experience that embodies the nation's rich gastronomic tradition.

Ingredients

  • Meat or seafood
  • Tamarind
  • Water
  • Kangkong
  • Radish
  • Eggplant
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the protein
  2. Boil the protein
  3. Add the vegeetables
  4. Add Tamarind Soup Base
  5. Add the any extra vegetables
  6. Taste and Adjust
  7. Serve
